Hello.

I am an artist and I am still learning and seeking new ways and means to broaden my knowledge, learn about the mysteries of life and grow out of every experience of life. I have immense love for art and I believe that art is not a profession to pursue; rather it is an understanding of oneself and life.

About

Current exploits

I'm currently pursuing my final year in Bachelor of Visual Arts (BVA) at Stella Maris, Chennai. I've been involved in projects related to painting, installations, sculptures, video art, pyrography and dabbling with various medium and fields during the course of my studies.

Source of inspiration

Art has been a part of my life since her childhood. To me, art is like meditation. I experiment a lot and have worked with a variety of media.

I often like to challenge my audience with provocative images. My practise begins by questioning social convention, the answer to which may or may not be given. But to me art is also about love, joy, and hope. While challenging conventions I also dwell in art that is invoked with a sense of hope.
